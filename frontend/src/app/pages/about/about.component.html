Our Project Story

The Vision

We believe AI should be more than just a tool—it should be an experience. Our vision is to make AI accessible, interactive, and adaptable, empowering creators to push boundaries and redefine engagement. Whether it’s for entertainment, automation, or innovation, we aim to bridge the gap between AI and creativity, giving you the power to shape the future!

The Team

We are Daan and Stijn, two 19-year-old software development students in our third year. Our shared passion for AI and technology brought us together to develop AI Stage. We believe in the power of AI to enhance entertainment and want to make it accessible to anyone looking for new ways to integrate technology into their work. With our combined knowledge and experience, we aim to build AI that is both user-friendly and adaptable.

Our Goals

Our goal is to make AI a powerful yet accessible tool for creators, streamers, and developers. We strive to build AI that seamlessly integrates into creative workflows, enhancing live interactions, automation, and entertainment. By allowing users to personalize their AI models, we enable unique experiences tailored to individual needs—pushing the boundaries of what AI can do.
